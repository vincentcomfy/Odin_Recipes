Ingredients

For the Ice Cream Base:
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
Mix-Ins (Choose Your Favorites):
  • Crushed Oreos
  • Chopped fruit (mango, banana, etc.)
  • Chocolate chips
  • Mini marshmallows
  • Peanut butter swirls

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Ice Cream Base:
    1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the heavy cream, sweetened condensed milk, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt until smooth and well combined.
  2. Add Your Mix-Ins:
    1. Gently fold in your chosen mix-ins, being careful not to over-mix.
    2. Feel free to go wild—this is your creation, after all!
  3. Chill the Mixture:
    1. Pour the mixture into an airtight container and spread it out evenly.
    2. Cover with a lid or plastic wrap, then freeze for at least 4 hours, or until fully set.
  4. Serve the Dirty Ice Cream:
    1. Once frozen, scoop out the ice cream into bowls or cones, letting it soften slightly for easier scooping.
    2. Top with additional mix-ins or drizzles if desired, and prepare for a flavor explosion!
